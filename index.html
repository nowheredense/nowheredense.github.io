About

I'm a mathematician graduating with a Ph.D. from Penn State University in December 2023. My thesis advisors were Jan Reimann and Linda Westrick. I also compose music and play the violin and viola da gamba.

Research interests

Logic, more specifically computability theory, Weihrauch complexity and computable combinatorics in reverse mathematics, computable analysis, probabilistic automata.
